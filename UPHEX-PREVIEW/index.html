    Campaign Details

    Campaign Details Instructions

    This section allows you to configure the basic settings for your Facebook campaign. Here you can set your campaign type, daily budget, and other essential parameters that will determine how your campaign operates.

    • Choose a campaign type that aligns with your marketing goals
    • Set an appropriate daily budget for your campaign
    • Provide a website URL where users will be directed after clicking your ad

    Custom Values

    Custom Values Instructions

    This section allows you to customize the variables in your template. These values will be used to personalize your ad content and make it more relevant to your audience.

    • Fill in all required fields marked with an asterisk (*)
    • Be descriptive and specific with your custom values
    • Preview how your values will appear in the ad by checking the preview panel

    Location Targeting Help

    Location Targeting Instructions

    This section allows you to define the geographic targeting for your Facebook campaign. You can select specific locations where your ads will be shown to reach your target audience.

    • Select locations where you want your ads to be displayed
    • Adjust the radius for each location to refine your targeting
    • Choose to include or exclude locations based on your targeting goals

    Advanced Settings Help

    Advanced Settings Instructions

    This section allows you to configure additional settings for your Facebook campaign. These settings are optional and can help you fine-tune your campaign.

    • Select an existing lead form or create a custom one
    • Choose a custom audience for retargeting
    • Select a pixel for website conversion tracking
